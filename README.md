# 🌍 Global Energy Transition & Sustainability Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Global Energy Transition & Sustainability Analysis is an end-to-end Data Analytics project focused on analyzing global energy consumption, renewable energy adoption, fossil fuel dependency, carbon intensity, and sustainability trends across countries using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

The project explores how countries are transitioning toward renewable energy while balancing economic growth and environmental sustainability. Python was used for data cleaning and preprocessing, SQL was used for analytical querying and trend analysis, and Power BI was used to build interactive dashboards and visualizations for business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Countries across the world are facing major sustainability and energy transition challenges due to:

* High dependency on fossil fuels
* Increasing carbon emissions and environmental impact
* Uneven renewable energy adoption across countries
* Rising energy demand and consumption
* Difficulty balancing economic growth with sustainability goals
* Lack of data-driven visibility into long-term energy transition trends

This project addresses these challenges through data-driven analytics and interactive dashboards.
